About

We believe that software can be an artform. With our passion for building beautiful, reliable software, we strive to make software that is not only cutting edge, but a joy to use. APB Software and Hardware has been operating since 2015 and is headquartered in Galway, Ireland. We provide full service software development and consultation with full-stack architecture design and development, graphic design, deployment and maintenance.

Our customers range from industry leading multinational corporations, to local boutique sole traders. Our service is built on excellent customer relations and close partnerships, so no matter how large your company, we can provide a top class service.
